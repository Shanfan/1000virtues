Contemporary Dance Class

My class focuses on creating a comfortable and safe yet challenging and exciting environment for dancers of any training, style or background to build trust and coordination of the body, space and music through structured improvisational exercises that encourage full body exploration of dynamics and texture, to master a solid foundation of technique through basic yoga, ballet and modern dance language and to deepen the fluidity and freedom of movement through the practice and refinement of intricate, luscious and sensual choreography.

Yoga with ChienHwe/1,000Virtues

I am trained in Hatha Vinyasa and I also completed Forrest Yoga Teacher Training Foundation course. My class focuses on pranayama, meditation, vipasana, core strength, holding poses and flow. I think every individual is coming from a different path in life and that should be honored and respected and even celebrated. Yoga is your spiritual union with your creator. There are many ways to connect to that. Breath work, meditation and yoga are some of the most powerful tools to do so. Both the yoga 101 and the classes allow you to connect to, listen to and build trust with your inner voice.

1,000Virtues/Yoga is offered through both private lessons and group classes.

Yoga 101

Yoga 101 begins with a consultation to assess needs and goals, a simple daily practice, a weekly phone call, weekly or monthly one-on-one sessions, unlimited email support and video instruction. Group Yoga

Krtajna Yoga, also know as “gratitude” yoga, begins with stillness, pranayama, meditation, moves into Forrest Yoga core strength, then flows into easy vinyasa with a different focus on a body part every class. We end class in savasana and close class by asking wise self for the most significant thing we received from class and for the most significant thing for which we feel gratitude, then send it back to the Universe.

1,000Virtues Master Class/Workshop

Dance Master Class

Master Class - A master class is the best way to introduce a new style, technique or idea to a body of students. Within a 2 to 3 hour class, students get the basics in an intense, challenging and motivating environment. Master classes are offered in contemporary dance, creative movement and yoga, meditation, dharma studies.

Length of Master Class: usually 2 hours, 1 day

Dance Workshop

Much like the master class, a workshop is a great way to not only introduce ideas, but to delve deeper into the practice with a longer time frame. Workshops are offered in Contemporary, creative movement, and yoga, meditation and dharma studies.

Length of workshops: 2-4 hours/day, 1-5 days

Dance Intensive

Intensives are the most challenging, yet most rewarding setting for which the student and the professional dancer to learn. By focussing on choreography, the participant is given the chance to master skills and nurture inner artistry. Available for dance studios and companies looking to expand movement vocabulary.

Length of dance intensives: 1-3 hours/day, 3-5 days

Yoga Intensive

Yoga intensives are a great way to pick one focus (breathing techniques, meditation, asana etc.) and refine and master the skill over a course of time together with the instructor. By extending the amount of time for practice from a few hours to over a few days, the participant gets a deeper practice right off the bat. Available for individuals, small groups, studios, offices.

Length of yoga intensive: 1-3 hours/day, 2-5 days

1,000Virtues is a home for contemporary dance training, performance and outreach, now fiscally sponsored by Boston Dance Alliance.
