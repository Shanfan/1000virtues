If you come to sign up a Yoga101 session...

Note that by initiating the procedure, you agree that 1,000 Virtues | Yoga 101 and Chien-Hwe is not responsible for any injuries or loss of personal items. You should understand that yoga is not a substitution for medical healthcare or any other necessary treatment that has been prescribed to you by your healthcare professionals.

After reviewing the above statement, still interested, please send me a free style email, covering these topics:

  • Your age and residential area
  • Yoga experience (classes, styles, retreats etc.)
  • Injury, pain, tension, tightness in your body
  • Are you taking an current medications? If so, what, and for how long?
  • What are you hoping to gain from Yoga 101 training?
  • Mental, emotional, physical and spiritual goals and intentions?

If you would like to book a workshop or master class,

Send me a free style email, and please let me know the information below:

  • Your organization and your desired time frame
  • What do you expect get from my service?
  • How did you hear about 1,000Virtues?

If you are considering to join the company,

The best way to show your interest in joining the company is for you to come and take my company class on Mondays from 5:45-7:15pm at Green Street Studios. You'll get a sense of my warm-up, movement/phrase work and choreography during this time. If we're compatible, then my hope is that I'll be able to work with you either as a paid, contracted dancer and founding member of the company, a guest dancer from project to project or even just in class.

Your Basic Info

1,000Virtues is named after its founder, Boston based choreographer, dancer and teacher, Chien-Hwe, whose name literally translates into a thousand virtues. It is a befitting name for a company dedicated to practicing, embodying and sharing the many virtues that dance has to offer.

1,000Virtues is a home for contemporary dance training, performance and outreach, now fiscally sponsored by Boston Dance Alliance.
